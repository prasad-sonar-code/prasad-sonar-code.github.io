About

I'm Prasad Sonar, a 2nd-year Electrical Engineering student at Gangamai College of Engineering, Dhule — going deep into DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering.

My engineering background gives me a systems-level mindset that most software-only developers don't have. I think about reliability, observability, and fault tolerance the same way an electrical engineer thinks about circuit resilience.
